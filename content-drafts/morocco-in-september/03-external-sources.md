## Note on festival and religious dates

Sources for the Imilchil Festival, L'Boulevard, Tanjazz, MOGA Festival, and Mawlid al-Nabi showed some variation between providers, and Islamic calendar dates (including Mawlid) depend on moon sighting and can shift by a day or more. **These specific festival dates were intentionally left out of the published article body** to avoid stating facts that could be wrong by the time a reader travels. If you want to add a "September Events" callout box to the article later, use this table as the sourced basis and add: *"Exact dates change annually — confirm with official Moroccan tourism sources before planning travel around any festival."*
